Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How do I book a room?
You can book online via our Rooms page, call us, or use the contact form. We recommend booking at least 3 days in advance for weekends.
What’s the cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your booking. Late cancellations may incur a 50% fee.
Do you offer food and drinks?
Yes! Full bar, signature cocktails, bottle service, and gourmet snacks. Outside food is not allowed.
Can I bring decorations for a party?
Absolutely! We allow balloons, banners, and cake. Let us know in advance so we can assist.
Is there an age restriction?
18+ after 8PM. Family-friendly hours on Sundays 5PM-8PM.
Do you have private parking?
Yes, a secured parking garage with validation for guests.
